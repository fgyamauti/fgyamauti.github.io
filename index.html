Fernando Yamauti

I'm a PhD student at Instituto de Matemática e Estatística (Universidade de São Paulo) and at Fakultät für Mathematik (Universität Regensburg).
I'm an associated member of the SFB 1085 - Higher Invariants.

I'm interested in geometry. For now, I'm mainly trying to understand three aspects of (higher) topos theory:

  • Topoi as generalised homotopy types;
  • Topoi as localic stacks;
  • Monoidal generalisations.
